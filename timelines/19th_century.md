# 19th Century Communication Timeline

## Tech Adoption Flow
```text
  [1837: Telegraph] -> [1844: Morse Msg] -> [1866: Transatlantic] -> [1876: Telephone] -> [1895: Radio]
```

- **1837:** Cooke and Wheatstone patent the electrical telegraph in England; Samuel Morse develops his version in the US.
- **1844:** First public telegraph message sent by Morse ("What hath God wrought").
- **1866:** Successful completion of the first permanent transatlantic telegraph cable.
- **1876:** Alexander Graham Bell patents the telephone.
- **1895:** Guglielmo Marconi demonstrates practical wireless telegraphy (radio).
