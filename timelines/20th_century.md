# 20th Century Communication Timeline

- **1920:** First commercial radio broadcast (KDKA in Pittsburgh).
- **1927:** First demonstration of television (Farnsworth).
- **1947:** Invention of the transistor at Bell Labs.
- **1969:** ARPANET, the precursor to the internet, connects the first four nodes.
- **1973:** First mobile phone call made by Martin Cooper (Motorola).
- **1989:** Tim Berners-Lee proposes the World Wide Web.
- **1990s:** Widespread adoption of cellular networks (2G) and the internet.
