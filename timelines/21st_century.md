# 21st Century Communication Timeline

## Tech Adoption Flow
```text
  [2007: Smartphone] -> [2009: 4G/LTE] -> [2010s: Streaming] -> [2019: 5G] -> [2020s: IoT/Satellite]
```

- **2007:** Apple introduces the iPhone, revolutionizing mobile data usage.
- **2009:** LTE (4G) technology begins commercial deployment.
- **2010s:** Widespread transition to streaming services (Netflix, Spotify) replacing broadcast/physical media.
- **2019:** 5G networks begin initial rollouts.
- **2020s:** Continued focus on low-latency, IoT (Internet of Things) integration, and satellite-based internet (Starlink).
