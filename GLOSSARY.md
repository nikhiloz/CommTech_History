# Glossary of Terms

- **Amplitude Modulation (AM):** A method of encoding data by varying the amplitude of the carrier signal.
- **Frequency Modulation (FM):** A method of encoding data by varying the frequency of the carrier signal.
- **Packet Switching:** A method of grouping data into packets that are transmitted over a network independently.
- **Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR):** The ratio of the strength of the desired signal to the background noise.
- **Telegraphy:** The long-distance transmission of messages where the sender uses symbolic codes, known to the recipient, rather than a physical exchange of an object bearing the message.
- **Transistor:** A semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electrical signals and power.
