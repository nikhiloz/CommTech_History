# Digital Era: Personal Computing

The revolution that brought digital power to individuals, changing the nature of work and home life.

## Key Developments
- **Microprocessors:** The integration of the CPU on a single chip, making affordable personal computers possible.
- **GUI (Graphical User Interface):** The shift from text-based interfaces to icons, windows, and mice, making computers accessible to non-experts.
- **Widespread Adoption:** Transformation of computing from a specialized enterprise task to a general consumer utility.
