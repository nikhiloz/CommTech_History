# Digital Era: Mobile Computing

The move toward portable, always-connected digital devices.

## Technical Mobile Computing Flow
```text
  [User Interface] <-> [App Processor/SoC] <-> [Cellular Modem/WiFi] --(Radio Wave)--> [Base Station/Internet]
                             |
                      [Power Management]
```

## Key Developments
- **Miniaturization:** Rapid advancements in hardware design, battery life, and display technology.
- **Always-on Connectivity:** Integration with cellular and WiFi networks, transforming devices from standalone tools into portals to global data.
- **Mobile Ecosystems:** The creation of app stores and platforms, fostering massive new markets and interaction models.
