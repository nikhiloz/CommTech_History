# Modern Era: Wireless Data

The proliferation of high-speed data networks, independent of cellular infrastructure.

## Key Developments
- **WiFi (IEEE 802.11):** Transforming home, office, and public connectivity by providing high-bandwidth wireless local area networking.
- **Bluetooth:** Enabling short-range, low-power connectivity between devices, forming the basis of the Internet of Things (IoT).
- **Satellite Data:** Modern low-earth-orbit (LEO) satellite constellations providing broadband internet to remote and underserved global regions.
