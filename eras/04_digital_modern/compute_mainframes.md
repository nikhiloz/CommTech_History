# Digital Era: Mainframes

Mainframes were the first digital workhorses, enabling complex data processing for large organizations.

## Technical Mainframe Architecture Flow
```text
  [Terminals/Clients] --(Comm Lines)--> [Front-End Processor] -> [Central Processor Unit (CPU)] <-> [Memory/Storage]
                                                                        |
                                                                 [Batch Controller]
                                                                        |
                                                                 [Peripheral Hub]
```

## Key Developments
- **Batch Processing:** Systems designed to handle large volumes of data in batches rather than interactive real-time.
- **Centralization:** Huge, centralized computing power serving thin-client terminals.
- **Foundational OS:** Development of early multi-user operating systems and complex job scheduling.
