# Technology: Transistors

The single most important component in modern digital electronics.

## Technical Transistor Switching Flow
```text
  [Gate Voltage (V_g)] -> [Electric Field Control] -> [Source Current (I_s)] -> [Drain Current (I_d)]
                                                            |
                                                   [Channel Modulation]
```

## Key Developments
- **Mechanism:** Solid-state semiconductor device that uses electrical voltage to control current flow.
- **Key Concepts:** Scaling (the ability to make them microscopic), low power consumption, extreme reliability, and incredible speed.
- **Impact:** Enabled Moore's Law, leading to the exponential growth in computing power.
